1894: Lucifer's Call

What is Lucifer's Call?

Chicago, 1894. Haunted by a curse casted by something dark and sinister, Holmes the hotel manage becomes obsessed with killing while experimenting on guests at his establishment. Ghouls from the other side, demons from another realm, and archangels with a thirst for human souls are creatures that run rampant at Holmes hotel. One detective has taken up the mandate upon himself to vanquish the evil and rid the world of the curse that keeps these supernatural beings bound to this world, but what he finds is much darker and eviler than any supernatural being he's ever faced.

7/10/2018 - Hello World, We are team 4 of CS2010. Here we plan to introduce to the world our visions and our game. At this date all gorup members were introduced to eachother and we met eachother for the first time. Starting off our team project we did not start with figuring out our idea however just focusing on the connecting our team through ice breaker challenges. This is an important step to establishing rapport between members and help when it came down to choosing the roles within the team.

12/10/2018

13/10/2018 - With our progress we have got the initial idea for our adventure game to be based around the game Cluedo however progressing more on the adventure basis with a character. We all agreed that we were going to have our games setting take place within a large mansion that will involve puzzle and exploration to get out alive (winning the game). When we had a clear idea of the visions of our game we began to make clear what we each wanted in our game and what our final product would look like. Key features which we established within the team included:

  • Puzzles
  • Inventory System which includes crafting
  • Enemies
  • Combat
